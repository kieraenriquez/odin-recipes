Low Calorie Protein Milkshake

Description

This delicious low calorie protein milkshake is a great volume drink to satisfy hunger for just 160 calories.

Ingredients

  • 240ml unsweetend almond milk
  • 30g vanilla protein powder
  • 20g fat-free vanilla pudding mix
  • ice
  • kosher salt

Instructions

  1. In a large blender, add almond milk, protein powder, fat-free vanilla pudding mix, and salt. Blend until mixed.
  2. Add ice and blend until there is a smooth fluffy consistency.

Low Calorie Pizza

Description

This delicious low calorie pizza is the perfect choice to satisfy cravings for 150 calories! Yields one serving.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 0.25tsp lemon juice
  • 30g light cream cheese
  • 30g tomato sauce
  • 30g light cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Separate the egg whites from the yolk and set the yolk aside. Whisk the egg whites and add lemon juice until stiff peaks form. Set aside.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine egg yolk and cream cheese until smooth.
  4. Fold egg yolk mixture into egg whites. Do not over mix. Ensure whites stay fluffy.
  5. Transfer mixture to prepared baking sheet. Spread mixture to form a circle.
  6. Bake for 25 minutes. Cool, add tomato sauce and cheese. Increase oven heat to 350 and then bake for another 10-15 minutes.
